About Myself

Growing up, I always dreamed of faraway places, vibrant cultures, and untold stories waiting to be discovered. After taking the plunge and leaving the comforts of home, I found my true passion in wandering through bustling cities, trekking remote landscapes, and immersing myself in the local way of life. Each trip is a new chapter in my story, and I'm here to share it with you.

When I landed in Bangkok, I had $1500 USD to my name and that was it. So I booked a $1 per night dorm room bed inside of a crumbling old wooden guesthouse in the center of the city and let the adventure begin from there. The funny thing is, that adventure is still going on today. In the end, I never went home after those three months.
